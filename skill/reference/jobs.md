# Jobs Flow

Emotional jobs-to-be-done: feeling outcomes users hire the product for.

## Handbook

Customer Discovery: `handbook/discovery/02-need-states.md` (circumstance, trigger, awareness). Pair with `01-ideal-customer-and-users.md`. Method: `05-interview-method.md`. Graduate via `06-synthesis.md`.

## Steps

1. Identify the situation that triggers use (real episode, not hypothetical).
2. Capture **before** emotion/tension and awareness stage if known.
3. Capture **during** desired emotion while working.
4. Capture **after** residue (pride, calm, competence, relief…).
5. Separate functional job from emotional job.
6. Rank emotional jobs by criticality for the north star.
