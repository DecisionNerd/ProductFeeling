# Peaks-Ends Flow

Apply the peak-end rule: remembered experience is dominated by the peak and the ending.

## Steps

1. Identify current peak (highest intensity moment) — intentional or accidental?
2. Identify ending of the session / flow / message.
3. Decide the **designed peak** (celebration, clarity, relief) aligned to the north star.
4. Design the **ending residue** (competence, calm, anticipation).
5. Demote accidental peaks (e.g. scary billing step should not be the emotional climax).

## Output

Peak, ending, demotions, and concrete craft notes for Impeccable.

Persist when `.productfeeling/` exists.
