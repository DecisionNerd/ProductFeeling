# Review Flow

Pre-ship emotional readiness gate.

## Gate questions

1. Is the primary feeling explicit and still intended?
2. Do the three highest-traffic moments match that feeling?
3. Are error and empty states emotionally acceptable?
4. Any anti-pattern or coercion risk?
5. Is the feeling executable by craft (Impeccable) without a new strategy debate?
6. Accessibility / reduced motion / plain-language needs respected?

## Verdict

- **Ship** — feeling is clear, moments designed, no ethical flags
- **Ship with changes** — list must-fix items
- **Hold** — north star missing, major cliffs, or ethical issues

## Output

Go/no-go with evidence, must-fix list, and optional follow-up techniques.

Persist to `.productfeeling/reviews/<slug>-review.md` when available.
