# Tools, Techniques, and Practices

Modular Tools, Techniques, and Practices (TTPs) you can combine under a strategy—or audit one at a time.

- [Calibrated Confidence](calibrated-confidence.md): When the product uses AI, show how sure it is—so people know when to trust the output and when to check it.
- [Commitment](commitment.md): Let users declare their intent, then design the product around it.
- [Contact Bridge](contact-bridge.md): Connect users to people they already know—without turning their address book into a growth hack.
- [Deep-link](deep-link.md): Drop users into the exact moment, feature, or context that matters.
- [Discovery](discovery.md): Guide users to value before they know where to look.
- [Effort Moat](effort-moat.md): Honour what people have built, so their progress remains useful and recognisable.
- [Empty States](empty-states.md): Turn “nothing here” into a clear, useful next step.
- [Fail Safe](fail-safe.md): Protect people from costly mistakes while keeping ordinary work moving.
- [Gamified Progress](gamified-progress.md): Make meaningful progress visible, achievable, and fully in the user’s control.
- [Graceful Exit](graceful-exit.md): Make leaving—cancelling, pausing, exporting, deleting—as respectful as joining, so the last impression earns the return visit.
- [Graceful Recovery](graceful-recovery.md): When the product fails, own it, explain it, and repair the user’s progress—so trust survives the bad day.
- [Intent Mirroring](intent-mirroring.md): Notice a meaningful signal, then offer a small, optional next step that fits the user’s likely goal.
- [Intentional Friction](intentional-friction.md): Add a small, purposeful pause when moving quickly could create harm, regret, or an avoidable mistake.
- [Investment](investment.md): Help users build something that becomes more useful because they shaped it.
- [JTBD Copywriting](jtbd-copywriting.md): Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD) copy: write from the progress the user is trying to make, not the feature your product wants to describe.
- [Limited Offer](limited-offer.md): Present a genuine, time-bound offer when it creates a fair choice, not pressure.
- [Loading Feedback](loading-feedback.md): Make unavoidable waits legible, calm, and proportionate.
- [Micro Interactions](micro-interactions.md): Small responses that make an action feel acknowledged, understandable, and complete.
- [Momentum Bias](momentum-bias.md): Give people a genuine head start without taking the decision away from them.
- [Pattern Alignment](pattern-alignment.md): Use familiar mental models where they help people act with confidence.
- [Perceived Effort Delay](perceived-effort-delay.md): When work takes time, show the real work; when it does not, respect the user’s time.
- [Permission Serve](permission-serve.md): Ask for access only when people can understand, choose, and benefit from it.
- [Personalisation](personalisation.md): Make the product fit the user’s stated needs—without making assumptions they cannot inspect or change.
- [Product Voice](product-voice.md): One recognisable voice, with a tone that adapts to the user’s moment—celebratory at wins, plain and calm at failures.
- [Progressive Disclosure](progressive-disclosure.md): Reveal the next useful layer of complexity when the user needs it, while keeping deeper capability easy to find.
- [Referral](referral.md): Help satisfied users invite people who will genuinely benefit, with a clear and respectful path for everyone.
- [Sandbox Experience](sandbox-experience.md): Let people experience a truthful slice of product value before asking them to commit.
- [Setup Defaults](setup-defaults.md): Give users a credible starting point they can understand, change, or skip.
- [Shareability](shareability.md): Help users share a meaningful outcome when they want to tell its story.
- [Small Quirk](small-quirk.md): A small, repeatable flourish that makes a clear product interaction feel recognisably yours.
- [Spark Curiosity](spark-curiosity.md): Offer a truthful glimpse of a useful outcome, then let users choose whether to explore it.
- [Success Moments](success-moments.md): Mark meaningful progress with feedback that feels earned, then let users decide what comes next.
- [System Widget](system-widget.md): Put one timely, glanceable piece of user-chosen value where people already look.
- [The Paywall](the-paywall.md): Present a clear, fair upgrade decision when users understand the value on offer.
- [Time to Value](time-to-value.md): Help people reach their first meaningful outcome with the least unnecessary effort.
- [Value Replay](value-replay.md): Bring a user’s meaningful progress back into view, only where and when they have chosen to receive it.
- [Variable Reward](variable-reward.md): Add optional novelty around reliable value, without making users chase uncertainty.
