# Product Strategies

Twelve strategies that combine Tools, Techniques, and Practices (TTPs) toward a product goal.

**Prerequisite:** [Customer Discovery](../discovery/index.md) — who this is for, what state they arrive in, how they work, talk, search, and buy. Shared vocabulary: [Concepts](../concepts/index.md).

- [Onboarding](01-onboarding.md): Get new users to first meaningful value with the least unnecessary ceremony.
- [Activation](02-activation.md): Get users to experience core value and become active.
- [Retention](03-retention.md): Earn return visits by making lasting value easy to resume.
- [Engagement](04-engagement.md): Increase depth of interaction without designing for compulsion.
- [Growth & Viral](05-growth-viral.md): Turn users into advocates and drivers of organic growth.
- [Monetisation](06-monetisation.md): Turn free or trial use into paid use when the upgrade feels fair and earned.
- [Conversion Optimisation](07-conversion-optimisation.md): Improve key conversion points without extractive pressure.
- [Experience Refinement](08-experience-refinement.md): Make competent products feel considered.
- [Trust Building](09-trust-building.md): Earn confidence through predictability, transparency, and protection.
- [Intent Shaping](10-intent-shaping.md): Invite users to declare intent, then design around it.
- [Habit Formation](11-habit-formation.md): Create sticky, repeated usage without designing for compulsion.
- [Premium Positioning](12-premium-positioning.md): Make the product feel high-value through earned craft and clarity.
