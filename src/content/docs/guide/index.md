## What ProductFeeling is

**ProductFeeling** decides how a product should *feel*. It sits upstream of **Impeccable** (craft) and beside **DocSlime** (docs).

## What it is not

Not a general UI polish skill (use Impeccable). Not clinical psychology. Not a toolkit for dark patterns.
