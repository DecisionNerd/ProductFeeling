# Market & users

## Market context

Product teams increasingly build with AI agent harnesses (Cursor, Claude Code, Codex, and peers). Those agents are strong at layout and weak at intentional affect. Demand is for **feeling-first guidance that fits the same install/skill workflow as craft and docs companions**.

## Primary users

People across the **product development lifecycle** who learn and apply feeling guidance:

| Segment | What they need from ProductFeeling |
|---|---|
| Product designers & PMs | Name the emotional north star; review flows/copy/states before polish |
| Engineers pairing with Impeccable | Emotion briefs that are executable without re-litigating strategy |
| Teams using DocSlime / `docs/` | Feeling woven into product, strategy, and experience docs — not a parallel SoT |

## Jobs (emotional / product)

- **Before:** Uncertainty or “it just feels off” with no shared language.
- **During:** Structured review against an intentional feeling; selective handbook depth when needed.
- **After:** Clear brief or durable docs update; handoff to craft or docs without bloat.

## Where strategy lives for this product

For **ProductFeeling itself**, durable strategic narrative lives in `docs/strategy/` (this folder). The skill should treat that tree as a primary workspace when improving ProductFeeling’s own product experience — not only when reviewing third-party apps.
